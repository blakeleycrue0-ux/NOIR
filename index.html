How we bake

Baked fresh, made by hand.

Every brownie is made to order in small batches from our kitchen in Mallorca. We don't mass-produce, we don't freeze, and we never let a brownie sit on a shelf — when your order comes in, that's when we start baking.

Only the good stuff. Real butter, fresh eggs and 72% dark chocolate. No palm oil, no artificial flavours, no shortcuts — just dense, fudgy, seriously chocolatey brownies.

A clean kitchen, every bake. Our workspace is cleaned and sanitised before and after every batch, ingredients are stored properly and checked for freshness, and everything is handled with real care. Got an allergy question? Ask before you order.

Straight to your door. Cooled, boxed in protective packaging and sent the same day, so it arrives fresh and intact.
